Card Games

Blackjack Instructions

  • Goal of the game is to get as close to 21 without going over
  • Face cards are 10 each, Ace is worth 11 points
  • The Hit button allows you to draw one random card
  • The Stand button ends your turn and allows the dealer to draw cards

War Instructions

  • Goal of the game is to get the highest value card
  • ACE = 14, KING = 13, QUEEN = 12, JACK = 11
  • Press draw to get a random card and the dealer will draw with you
  • If you go on a winstreak, your longest winstreak gets recorded!
